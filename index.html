Disney Imagineer Competition

Participated in a Disney Imagineer Competition. I participated with my friends Joseph Kim and Claire Ku. We designed a company called LAPS. The purpose of the app was to better the structure of the public transport system within LA! We would have various hubs that would connect to a main hub. Shuttles were used to connect these hubs to conduct easier transport within the busy city and reduce traffic. This would lead to less carbon emission within our atmosphere and overall a more happier, public transport efficient city. I was in charge of the 3D model design of the trains and the app configuration, Claire created all the drawings of where our hubs would be and found their proper locations in correlation with an IRL map, and Joe created our project proposal/story.
